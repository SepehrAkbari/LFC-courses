Introducing Currency Convert Rate

Definition: A currency conversion rate, often referred to simply as an "exchange rate," is the ratio at which one currency can be exchanged or converted into another currency. It represents the value of one currency in terms of another and is typically expressed as a numerical value. Exchange rates fluctuate continuously in response to various factors, including economic conditions, geopolitical events, and market sentiment.
